Sushi Bar Maumi

Sushi Bar Maumi is offered by owner/chef and his wife. Every night, Maumi only offers 3 rounds of set-dinner omakase, each round with limited at-the-bar seating. Shipping their ingredeints straight from Japan, Maumi offers fresh selections every day with authentic choices not commonly found in Vancouver.
