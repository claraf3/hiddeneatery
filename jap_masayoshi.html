Masayoshi

Located in East Vancouver, Mayayoshi is operated by Japanese chefs and offers al-la-carte menu as well as a few types of omakase. Seats are offered at the bar as well as regular tables. Omakase choices includes sushi-only, as well as an omakase including other delicately prepared hot dishes.
