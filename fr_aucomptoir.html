Au Comptoir

Au Comptoir is both a bistro and a cafe in that it is a great choice to grab a coffee with a friend as well as amazing brunches on the weekends and authentic french cuisine in the evening. One of its brunch highlights is the perfectly juicy burger with melted gryuere and pan-seared foie gras.
