Unchai Restaurant

Operated by owner/chef, Unchai offers authentic thai dishes with a twist. Unchai is a small shop located in Burrard Street that is often found packed at night. Popular dishes include boat noodles, pad thai, fried chicken wings, and mango sticky rice.

  • Location: 2351 Burrard St, Vancouver, BC V6J 3J2
