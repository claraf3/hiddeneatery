Linh Cafe

Linh Cafe serves up uniquely flavored French-Vietnamese fusion dishes. Traditional style pho can be found along side special French-style dishes such as chicken liver, escargot, and white wine mussels. Some special dishes such as whole-roasted pig are also up for order ahead of time.
