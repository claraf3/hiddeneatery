Sen Pad Thai

Located inside the food court of Granville Island market, its made-to-order stir fry rice, noodles, and pad thai are fresh and flavorful. Although it is no traditional restaurant, its authentic Thai food combined with their famous Thai milk tea is one of the best meals one can ask for.
