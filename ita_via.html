Via Tevere Pizzeria

Via Tevere Pizzeria is another Italian Pizzeria that offers made-to-order wood fired pizzas. Long lines can often be found outside Via Tevere during busy hours at night. Aside from its famous pizzas, its tiramisu dessert is also to die for.
