Absinthe Bistro

Run by a husband-wife duo with the husband who was trained in France as the chef, Absinthe Bistro serves a rotating menu comprised of modern french cuisine. Famous for its scallops and absinthe drip, Absinthe Bistro is an excellent choice for patrons looking for quality time and good food.
